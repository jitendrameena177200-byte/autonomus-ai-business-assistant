Trident Group — Executive Leadership Meeting Minutes
Date: September 18, 2026
Attendees: CEO, COO, CFO, VP Sales, VP Engineering, VP People

1. Q3 Financial Check-in
CFO presented preliminary Q3 numbers showing revenue tracking approximately
4% ahead of forecast, led by Industrial Equipment. CFO flagged that gross
margin compressed by 1.1 points due to elevated freight costs and requested
that Operations evaluate alternate freight carriers before Q4 budgeting
begins.

2. TridentFlow Product Line
VP Engineering reported that the TridentFlow pump line has a defect rate of
0.8%, within target, but the current manufacturing partner in Malaysia is
at 92% capacity utilization. The team is evaluating a second manufacturing
partner in Thailand to add redundancy before Q1 FY2027, with a decision
targeted for late October.

3. Consumer Hardware Division
Following two quarters of missed targets, the leadership team agreed to
commission a 30-day strategic review of the Consumer Hardware division.
Options under consideration include repositioning the product line toward
the small-business segment, licensing the brand to a third-party
manufacturer, or winding the division down by Q2 FY2027. VP Sales will
lead the review with support from Finance.

4. Hiring Plan
VP People presented the Q4 hiring plan: 14 open requisitions, concentrated
in Southeast Asia sales (5), supply chain (4), and software engineering (5)
to support the new internal tooling initiative. Budget approved contingent
on Q3 numbers finalizing above forecast.

5. Southeast Asia Expansion
VP Sales confirmed the Vietnam and Indonesia distribution partnerships are
ahead of schedule, with first shipments completed in August. The team
proposed evaluating a Philippines partnership in Q1 FY2027 given early
regional demand signals.

6. Action Items
- CFO: circulate updated freight-cost analysis by September 30.
- VP Engineering: present Thailand manufacturing partner evaluation at the
  October 20 leadership meeting.
- VP Sales: deliver Consumer Hardware strategic review findings by
  October 18.
- VP People: begin sourcing for approved Q4 hiring plan immediately.

Next meeting: October 20, 2026.
