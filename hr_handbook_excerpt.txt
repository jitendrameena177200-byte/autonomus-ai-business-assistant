Trident Group — Employee Handbook (Excerpt)
Section 4: Leave and Time Off Policy
Last updated: January 2026

4.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees accrue PTO based on tenure:
- 0-2 years of service: 15 days per year
- 3-5 years of service: 20 days per year
- 6+ years of service: 25 days per year

PTO accrues monthly and may be carried over up to a maximum of 10 unused days
into the following calendar year. Any unused balance beyond 10 days is
forfeited on December 31, unless local law requires otherwise.

4.2 Sick Leave
Employees receive 8 paid sick days per year, separate from PTO. Sick leave
does not carry over between years. Employees who are out for more than 3
consecutive days must provide a physician's note to HR.

4.3 Parental Leave
Trident Group provides 12 weeks of paid parental leave for the primary
caregiver and 6 weeks for the secondary caregiver, applicable to birth,
adoption, and foster placement. Parental leave must be taken within 12
months of the qualifying event and can be split into two blocks with
manager approval.

4.4 Remote Work Policy
Employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject
to manager approval and role requirements. Fully remote arrangements require
Director-level sign-off and are reviewed every 6 months. All remote
employees must remain reachable during core hours of 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM in
their assigned office's local time zone.

4.5 Requesting Time Off
All time-off requests must be submitted through the Workday portal at least
5 business days in advance, except in emergencies. Manager approval is
required before time off is considered confirmed. Employees should not book
non-refundable travel until a request has been formally approved.

4.6 Holidays
Trident Group observes 11 paid company holidays annually. The full holiday
calendar is published each December for the following year on the internal
HR portal.
